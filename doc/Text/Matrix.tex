\subsection{Matrix  class}
Mathematical matrix
\begin{lstlisting}[language=C++]

\end{lstlisting}
\textbf{Template Parameters} \\ 
\textit{T} Type of matrix \\ 
\textit{N} Row \\ 
\textit{M} Column \\
